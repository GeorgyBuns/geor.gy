Freqeuntly Asked Questions 🤔

What’s the average cost of the project?

I work with clients from all over the world. Every country has different financial situation, same goes for businesses. I take many things into consideration before I start working with someone — including one’s financial situation.
‍
That being said, I have a certain point when I cannot decrease costs of my services, because of the financial situations I talked above — I too have certain situation and lifestyle as everybody does. This point varies from country to country. Currently, it’s around $500, but it goes up every month.

What costs should I expect other than your design services?

Most basic projects such as simple one page websites require at least a hosting and domain. When you need more sophisticated functionality and features: API connections, e.g. Zapier or Integromat, online forms or surveys. You’ll need to pay for an ability to publish apps on Google Play or App Store if you need a native mobile app. Also, it’s necessary to have privacy policy and similar documents depending on your country — it’s a legal requirement.

What if I don’t have the money for your services?

In a few rare occasions, I can help you with your project for free on certain conditions. The same goes for NGOs — I’ll be glad to support your important cause you are fighting for!
‍
That being said, please do not send me emails asking for free help if you are not a NGO or your project is just a copycat of a popular product.

What if I’m not sure I’m ready to make this investment?

Money is personal and it takes many difficult decisions to invest in something — I’m an entrepreneur who runs a small business too!
‍
The key question I would ask myself before making an investment decision: what if I could get new clients faster and make return on investment in a span of a few months? This is just a no brainer for me — my business isn’t growing if I don’t get new clients each month. Pre-existing clients who continuously choose me over other executors are great (love ‘em!), but I need more clients to grow the business.

What if I’m not sure that I need a website?

This is quite common question amongst business owners, especially smaller ones.
‍
I do offer free half an hour call, so you can tell me about your business, and I’ll be able to help you find out if you really need a website. Maybe it will be enough for you to have presence on social media — for example, there are many business that only have Instagram profile. However, having a website expands your business capabilities: you can sell products on it, have a blog, showcase and present products in a way you want, all the design in your hands.

Which platform will you use for my project?

I create websites on Webflow — a very powerful design tool that can be used to develop almost anything you need. Sometimes, I use Carrd, Readymag, Squarespace or Tilda when my clients need to get a website really fast and they don’t mind limitations of these two platforms.
‍
As for apps: I use Bravo, AppGyver and Glide. These design tools allow me to create and deliver great PWAs and native mobile apps fast!

Who owns the content on my app or website?

You own everything! I transfer ownership of your app or website over to you when we finish working on your project. I also give you the rights to the intellectual property such as images or videos that I created for your project.

Do you offer ongoing support after the project is over?

At the end of the project, we can discuss how we can continue working together to make your website even better.

Where are you based right now?

Currently, I reside in my hometown in Russia — GMT+3 (Moscow Time) time zone. Sometimes the information here isn’t updated and I might be in another places, but worry not — I work with clients from all over of the world, from  San Francisco to Tokyo, from Moscow to Hong Kong.

When can we get started?

‍I’m available for new projects as for right now. I tend to work with just a few clients, so I won’t be overwhelmed with work. Also, it helps me focus on my clients’ projects more.
